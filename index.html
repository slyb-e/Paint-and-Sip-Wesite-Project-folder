What is Paint and Sip?

"Paint and Sip" is a social event where participants create a painting while enjoying drinks, typically wine. Guests follow step-by-step guidance to complete their own unique artwork, making it a popular activity for group outings, parties, or creative relaxation.

Where did paint and sip come from?

2007

Cathy Deano and Renee Maloney founded the first paint and sip studio, Painting with a Twist, in Mandeville, Louisiana.

2010's

Painting with a Twist expanded rapidly, opening franchise locations across the U.S.

2016

Paint and sip went global, with studios opening in cities worldwide. Companies introduced themed events, virtual paint nights, and wine pairings.
